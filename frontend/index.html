How it works

One ledger, two sides of the same agreement.

Every ISA is a single on-chain record. Earners and investors read from the same source of truth — what's owed, what's been paid, and what's left.

For earners

Raise without debt

01

Set your terms. Choose how much to raise, your income share, and the repayment cap.

02

Publish the ISA. Terms are written to the registry contract — visible, immutable, not negotiable after the fact.

03

Get funded. Investors deposit USDC into your pool until the target is reached.

04

Repay from income. Submit proof of earnings; the contract distributes your share pro-rata, and stops the moment your cap is hit.

For investors

Back people, not balance sheets

01

Browse live ISAs. Every listing shows the earner's share, term, and cap before you commit a cent.

02

Fund a pool. Your deposit is tracked as shares in the funding pool contract — no intermediary holds your position.

03

Track repayments. Watch income land and distribute automatically as the earner reports.

04

Exit at the cap. Once the repayment cap is reached, the contract closes the position — by design, not by negotiation.

Why Stellar

Built for settlement speed and low fees, not speculation.

An ISA only works if repayments clear fast and cheaply, month after month. That's a Stellar problem to solve, not a general-purpose-chain one.

Soroban

Three small contracts, not one monolith

Registry, funding pool, and repayment distributor are separated so each can be audited and reasoned about on its own.

Freighter

Sign with the wallet you already use

Network detection and a testnet warning banner mean you always know which ledger you're signing against.

Indexer

Every event, mirrored to a queryable record

A lightweight indexer polls Stellar RPC and mirrors pool_fund and repaid events into the explorer in near real time.

Your first ISA takes about four minutes to set up.
