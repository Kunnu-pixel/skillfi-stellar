# SkillFi: 10-User Onboarding & Feedback Plan

To validate our platform and satisfy the Level 4 MVP grant/hackathon criteria, we must coordinate testing with a minimum of 10 real users on the Stellar Testnet. This document outlines our recruitment strategy, user instructions, feedback questionnaire, and tracking registry template.

---

## 1. Recruitment Strategy & Channels
*   **Stellar Discord/Developer Channels**: Pitch the product in the `#dev-discussion` or `#showcase` channels.
*   **Web3 & University FinTech Clubs**: Reach out to university blockchain clubs or student freelancer communities who stand to benefit most from ISAs.
*   **Twitter/X Tech & Hackathon Communities**: Post a short pitch and request builders to do a test run of a next-generation education funding platform.

---

## 2. User Setup Guide (Sent to Testers)
Provide testers with the following simple checklist to get started:
1.  **Install Freighter Wallet**: Install the extension from [Freighter.com](https://www.freighter.com/).
2.  **Switch to Testnet**: Open Freighter settings -> Preferences -> Network, and verify it is set to **Testnet**.
3.  **Fund Your Account**: Go to the [Stellar Laboratory Friendbot](https://laboratory.stellar.org/#account-creator?network=testnet) and paste your Freighter public key to get testnet XLM.
4.  **Connect to SkillFi**: Visit the deployed demo URL, click **Connect Wallet**, and sign the connection message.

---

## 3. Testing Scenarios

We will divide our 10 testers into two roles:

### Group A: Earners (5 Users)
*   **Task 1**: Create a profile indicating their educational skills (e.g. Web3 Dev, UI/UX Designer) and funding goals.
*   **Task 2**: Submit an ISA proposal requesting 1,000 USDC, pledging 5% of their monthly income for 12 months (repayment cap of 1.5x, i.e., 1,500 USDC).
*   **Task 3**: Request verification of a mock income report and execute a mock repayment via the dashboard.

### Group B: Investors (5 Users)
*   **Task 1**: Browse the open ISA requests on the transparency explorer.
*   **Task 2**: Fund an open ISA proposal by depositing testnet USDC.
*   **Task 3**: View the newly minted claim shares on their investor portfolio dashboard.
*   **Task 4**: Track repayments distributed to their wallet.

---

## 4. Feedback Questionnaire
Upon completing the scenarios, users will submit feedback via an embedded feedback form (linked to our backend or a free Typeform/Google Form) containing these questions:
1.  **Clarity of Value**: On a scale of 1-5, how clear is SkillFi's value proposition compared to traditional student/freelance loans?
2.  **Usability**: How easy was it to navigate the dashboard, connect Freighter, and execute transactions? (Include any friction points).
3.  **Real-World Intent**: Would you use this platform for real funding (as an earner) or yield generation (as an investor)? Why/Why not?
4.  **Security & Trust**: What concerns, if any, do you have regarding the safety of the smart contracts or income verification?
5.  **Suggestions**: What features or integrations are missing that would make this product complete?
